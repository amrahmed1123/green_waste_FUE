How to Recycle in 3 Easy Steps

Separate Your Waste

Sort your trash into recycling, compost, and landfill bins

Check for Recycling Symbols

Look for the recycling symbol on packaging to see if it's recyclable

Recycle Properly

Make sure your recyclables are clean, dry, and free of contaminants

Benefits of Recycling

Reduces Landfill Waste

Recycling diverts waste from landfills, reducing the amount of trash that ends up in the environment

Conserves Natural Resources

Recycling conserves natural resources like trees, water, and minerals

Reduces Greenhouse Gas Emissions

Recycling uses less energy than producing new products, which reduces greenhouse gas emissions

Recycling Tips

  • Recycle paper, cardboard, glass, and plastic bottles and containers
  • Reuse bags and containers instead of throwing them away
  • Donate or sell unwanted items instead of throwing them away
  • Recycle electronics at designated recycling centers
  • Compost food waste instead of throwing it away
